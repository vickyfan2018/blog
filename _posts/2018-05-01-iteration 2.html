Microinteraction

Clicked button animation

To add more fun to use the website, I added a button animation, which when users click on the button, the white area will extend from the clicked area to the entire button background


Scribble animation on scroll

I added a scribble animation, which the sketches will be scribbled when the users scroll the page. When users scroll over the entire section, the whole sketches will be appeared.


In order to make the scribble effect, I added a svg polyline code exported from illustrator, overlaying on my sketches.


In order to animate the effects when users scroll to the about us section, I used the MagicScroll and TweenMax js plungin to animate the svg line overlayed on the sketches.
